# Asset Guide — What Goes Where

## Landing Page
**`landing/videos/`** — Highlight reel clips for the hero background
- Drop 4-5 short clips (10s each) of Man City highlights
- Format: .mp4 (h264), keep under 5MB each for fast loading
- Name them: `highlight-1.mp4`, `highlight-2.mp4`, etc.
- These will auto-loop/cycle behind the hero text with a dark overlay

**`landing/backgrounds/`** — Static fallback images for the landing hero
- A hero image for when video isn't available
- Stadium wide shot, team celebration, etc.
- Suggested: 1920x1080 minimum

## Dashboard
**`dashboard/backgrounds/`** — Subtle background textures or images
- Hero carousel slide backgrounds (could be player shots, stadium, training)
- Name by slide: `slide-1.jpg`, `slide-2.jpg`, etc.

## Games Hub
**`games/tiles/`** — Game tile artwork (one per game)
- `ea-fc-26.jpg` — EA FC key art or Man City player in EA FC
- `roblox-blue-moon.jpg` — Blue Moon experience art / Moonchester
- `fortnite.jpg` — Fortnite x Man City crossover imagery
- `rocket-league.jpg` — Rocket League with Man City decal
- `fc-online.jpg` — FC Online key art
- Suggested: 600x400 or similar landscape ratio

**`games/backgrounds/`** — Optional page background for Games Hub

## Leaderboard
**`leaderboard/backgrounds/`** — Optional background imagery
- Could use a subtle stadium/crowd shot with heavy dark overlay

## Rewards
**`rewards/icons/`** — Monochromatic reward icons (if custom beyond Lucide)
- SVG format preferred
- White or sky blue on transparent

**`rewards/backgrounds/`** — Optional page background

## Onboarding
**`onboarding/backgrounds/`** — Subtle background for onboarding flow

## Admin
**`admin/backgrounds/`** — Background for the admin/backend view

## Shared Assets (used across multiple screens)
**`shared/logos/`** — Man City crests, logos, wordmarks
- `man-city-crest.svg` or `.png` — Primary crest (used in sidebar, landing)
- `man-city-wordmark.svg` — Text logo if available
- Keep transparent background

**`shared/icons/`** — Any custom monochromatic icons not in Lucide
- Platform icons (PS5, Xbox, Switch, PC, Mobile) if you want custom ones
- Social platform icons if preferred over text abbreviations

**`shared/players/`** — Player images for use across the site
- Cutout PNGs of key players (Haaland, Foden, etc.)
- Transparent backgrounds
- Used in hero sections, reward cards, tier celebrations

**`shared/sponsors/`** — Sponsor logos (monochromatic versions)
- `puma.svg` — Puma logo (white or sky blue on transparent)
- `etihad.svg` — Etihad Airways logo
- `asahi.svg` — Asahi Super Dry logo
- Keep them monochromatic to match the dark theme
